# Privacy Policy for AetherScan

AetherScan is a Firefox extension that provides nmap diagnostic functionality by communicating with a local native messaging host.

## Data Collection and Usage
AetherScan does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or analytics to any external servers or third parties. 

All operations, including nmap scans and data processing, are performed entirely locally on your machine. The extension requires the "tabs" permission solely to determine the hostname of the active tab for scanning convenience, and the "storage" permission to save your scan history locally within your browser.

## Native Messaging
AetherScan uses the "nativeMessaging" permission to communicate with a local Python script installed on your computer. This script strictly executes nmap commands as requested by the extension and returns the results back to the extension. No data is sent over the internet or shared with anyone.

## Changes to This Policy
If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update this document accordingly. Since we do not collect any user data, we do not require any mechanism to notify users of changes other than updating the extension documentation.
